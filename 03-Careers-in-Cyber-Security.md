# Careers in Cyber Security

## Summary

In this TryHackMe room, I explored various career paths within the cybersecurity industry. I learned about different roles such as Security Analyst, Penetration Tester, Security Engineer, Incident Responder, and Digital Forensics Investigator. The room explained the responsibilities, skills, and career opportunities available in each role. This helped me gain a better understanding of the cybersecurity field and potential career directions.

## Key Learnings

- Learned about different cybersecurity career paths.
- Understood the responsibilities of Security Analysts and Penetration Testers.
- Gained insight into Security Operations Centers (SOCs).
- Learned the importance of continuous learning in cybersecurity.
- Explored the skills required for various cybersecurity roles.
