# Kenobi

## Summary

I completed the Kenobi room on TryHackMe, a beginner-friendly CTF focused on network enumeration, service exploitation, and Linux privilege escalation. The room provided hands-on experience with identifying exposed services, gathering information, gaining access, and escalating privileges.

## Key Learnings

- Network Enumeration
- SMB Enumeration
- NFS Misconfiguration
- SSH Key Discovery
- Linux Privilege Escalation
- Service Enumeration
