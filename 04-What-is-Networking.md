# What is Networking?

## Summary

In this TryHackMe room, I learned the fundamentals of computer networking and how devices communicate with each other. The room introduced concepts such as IP addresses, MAC addresses, routers, switches, and protocols. I also learned how data travels across networks and how networking forms the foundation of modern communication and cybersecurity. This room provided a strong introduction to networking concepts that are essential for cybersecurity professionals.

## Key Learnings

- Learned the basics of computer networking.
- Understood the purpose of IP and MAC addresses.
- Learned how routers and switches help devices communicate.
- Gained an introduction to network protocols.
- Understood why networking knowledge is important in cybersecurity.
